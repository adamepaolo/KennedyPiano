Meet Our Teachers

Dedicated and experienced educators who love to teach.

Lana Tchernaia

Founder & Piano Instructor

Lana is a third-generation teacher from Siberia, Russia, with over 25 years of teaching experience. She holds a Bachelor of Music in Piano Performance and Pedagogy and combines traditional Russian methods with her unique pedagogy.

Jotham

Piano Instructor

A pianist and producer, Jotham customizes lessons for students based on their goals, from RCM exams to songwriting. He is currently studying at Humber College.

Afshin

Cello, Violin, Viola, and Piano Instructor

Afshin has a decade of teaching experience and is an Advanced Orff Instructor. He has published a lesson book and created a music album for children.

Suzanne

Piano Instructor

Suzanne is a dedicated piano teacher who combines her love for music with her passion for teaching. She holds a Bachelor of Music and is an expert in preparing students for competitions and examinations.

Sep

Piano & Guitar Instructor
