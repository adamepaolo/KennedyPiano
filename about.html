About Us

Our story, our mission, and our values.

Welcome to Kennedy Piano Studio, also known as Scarborough School of Music

Established in 2010, our studio has been a pillar of the Toronto music community for over a decade. We believe in providing a personalized approach to music education, nurturing a love for music that lasts a lifetime. Our goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where every student, regardless of age or skill level, can flourish.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, personalized music instruction to students of all ages and abilities. We aim to inspire creativity, foster discipline, and build confidence through the joy of making music. We cater to all types of learners, from recreational music-makers to those pursuing high-level examinations with the Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) and Conservatory Canada.

Our Commitment

  • Personalized Instruction: We tailor every lesson to the individual student's goals, pace, and learning style, including popular skills like playing by ear, improvising, and sight-reading. Lessons are available in English, French, and Russian.
  • Experienced Teachers: Our instructors are not only skilled musicians but also dedicated educators who are passionate about teaching.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: We cover everything from technical skill and music theory to creative expression and performance practice.
  • A Welcoming Community: We strive to create a warm and inclusive space where students feel safe to explore, make mistakes, and celebrate their progress.
