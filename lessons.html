Piano Lessons

Our comprehensive piano program is tailored to students of all levels, from beginners to advanced players. We focus on building a strong foundation in technique and theory while also encouraging creative expression through improvisation and sight-reading. Our students are well-prepared for recitals, competitions, and examinations with the Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) and Conservatory Canada.

  • Comprehensive theory and technique training
  • Preparation for RCM and Conservatory Canada exams
  • Focus on both classical and contemporary repertoire
  • Opportunities for recitals and performances
