Music Instructor Positions

The Scarborough School of Music, also known as Kennedy Piano Studio, is looking for passionate and experienced music instructors to join our team. We're seeking individuals with a strong background in their instrument and a genuine desire to help students achieve their musical goals.

Qualifications:

  • Degree or diploma in music education or performance
  • Minimum 2 years of teaching experience
  • Proficiency in at least one of the following instruments: piano, violin, cello, guitar, or drums
  • Patience, enthusiasm, and strong communication skills

Responsibilities:

  • Provide comprehensive music instruction
  • Be on time and friendly to students
  • Be enthusiastic and proactive in teaching

How to Apply:

Please send your resume to: kps.hires@gmail.com
