Music Instructor Positions

The Scarborough School of Music, also known as Kennedy Piano Studio, is currently seeking enthusiastic and dedicated music instructors for our growing studio. We are located just a 5-minute walk from Kennedy Subway station.

Open Positions:

  • Guitar Instructor
  • Violin Instructor
  • Piano Instructor

Requirements:

  • Some knowledge of music pedagogy
  • Strong desire to help students of all ages and abilities
  • B.A. or B.M. in music, or close to completion
  • Minimum of 2 years of music teaching experience
  • Ability to teach beginner through intermediate students
  • Excellent communication skills

Responsibilities:

  • Provide comprehensive music instruction
  • Be on time and friendly to students
  • Be enthusiastic and proactive in teaching

How to Apply:

Please send your resume to: kps.hires@gmail.com
